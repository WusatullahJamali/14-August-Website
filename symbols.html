National Symbols

Flag, crest, birds, and the anthem — the heart of a nation.

National Flag

Green field with a white vertical stripe at the hoist, a white crescent, and a five-pointed star. Green symbolizes the Muslim majority; white represents minorities and peace; the crescent signifies progress, and the star denotes light & knowledge.

Green & White Crescent Five-Point Star

National Details

  • Capital: Islamabad
  • Largest City: Karachi
  • Official Language: Urdu (English widely used)
  • Currency: Pakistani Rupee (PKR)
  • Motto (informal): Faith, Unity, Discipline

National Bird

The Chukar Partridge is recognized as the national bird — a symbol of rugged beauty found in Pakistan’s hills and highlands.

Chukar Partridge (Alectoris chukar)

National Anthem

Poetry by Hafeez Jalandhari; composition by Ahmad G. Chagla. The anthem evokes devotion to the homeland and divine guidance.

پاک سرزمین شاد باد

کشورِ حسین شاد باد
تو نشانِ عزمِ عالی شان
ارضِ پاکستان! مرکزِ یقین شاد باد
